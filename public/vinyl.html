Questions

Vinyl FAQ

Is this pressed vinyl?

No. These are individually lathe-cut records, suited to one-offs and very small quantities.

How much music fits?

It depends on the record size, speed, level and bass content. Shorter sides generally allow a stronger, cleaner cut. A duration calculator is planned for a future update.

Can you cut any audio?

The audio must be owned or properly authorised by you. I’ll also check whether the master is technically suitable before confirming the job.

What is the turnaround?

Turnaround information will be confirmed with each quote while the cutting workflow is being established.
