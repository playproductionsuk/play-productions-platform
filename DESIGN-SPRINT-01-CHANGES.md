# Design Sprint 01 changes

## Public experience

- Replaced the Phase 1.2 desktop sidebar with a symmetrical public header: navigation left, centred Play Productions logo, cart/account/DJ access right.
- Preserved a contained mobile menu and added active states, focus rings, restrained lift/glow interactions and consistent content widths.
- Reordered the homepage into hero, trust strip, latest music, service paths and the premium footer.
- Removed the duplicated latest-release block from the Music Store.
- Added playable previews, More Details links and persistent Add to Cart controls.
- Added a cart drawer foundation shared by music now and future services, vinyl and merch.
- Ranked related tracks by genre, BPM proximity and shared mood tags.
- Reworked DJ promo browsing to resemble the public store while retaining protected WAV downloads and no purchase actions.
- Rebuilt the footer with Play Productions positioning, navigation, Follow and Listen groups using the existing social icons with lime treatment.

## Services, vinyl and portals

- Connected the mixing quote builder to owner-editable pricing settings.
- Added the complete mixing project workflow and retained before/after published case studies.
- Expanded vinyl requests with sleeve, label, sample-cut and audio-upload options plus a configurable custom-enquiry quantity threshold.
- Made customer portal project navigation conditional and added newsletter preferences.

## Business Dashboard

- Renamed and restyled the owner area as a full-width Business Dashboard with top navigation.
- Added clickable operational cards and dedicated routes for Music Library, Mixing Projects, Vinyl Projects and DJ Access.
- Expanded release metadata with ISRC, UPC, TuneCore, PRS, PPL, Spotify, Apple Music, MP3 and WAV fields.
- Added track-style case-study upload for cover, before audio, after audio, artist, track, description and testimonial.
- Expanded Settings into brand, homepage, pricing, social, SEO, page visibility, payments and email groups.

## Assets

- Added the documented `public/assets/` category structure for final replacement media.

## Preserved

Firebase, Firestore rules, authentication, individual DJ approvals, customer/admin foundations, payments foundation, multi-page routing, track health, SEO and existing project/order workflows remain in place.
