About Play Productions

Play Productions is an independent music producer creating original music across UK Garage, Grime, Hip Hop, Jungle, Drum & Bass and experimental electronic music.

The journey began at around eleven years old with a pair of bedroom turntables and a passion for underground music. By the mid-teens, Play Productions had become part of a local UK Garage crew, producing mixtapes for the local scene before stepping behind the microphone as an MC. That experience led to performing in clubs from the age of seventeen, developing an understanding of music that could only be learned from playing to real crowds.

Today, that same passion has evolved into music production. In recent years, the focus has been on writing, producing, mixing and mastering original music, combining decades of experience with modern production techniques and a commitment to quality over quantity.

Whether producing original releases, collaborating with other artists or providing professional mixing and mastering services, the goal has always remained the same: to create music with authenticity, energy and attention to detail.
